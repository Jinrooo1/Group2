Streaming Software that we will use




We brainstormed and discussed the pros and cons of each broadcasting software. We all gave our own opinions and ideas about certain broadcasting software. When comparing the pros and cons of broadcasting software, our group concluded that OBS Studio was the best. Additionally, we were able to compile a list of every piece of streaming gear. By utilizing OBS, we will be broadcasting a podcast-style program in which we will discuss our research and thoughts on depression and how gaming can affect it!
